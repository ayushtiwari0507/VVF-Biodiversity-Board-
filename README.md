# 🌿 VVF Biodiversity Dashboard – Taloja Site

## About the Project

The **VVF Biodiversity Dashboard** is an interactive digital platform developed to showcase the biodiversity present within and around the premises of **VVF India Limited – Taloja**, a leading oleochemical manufacturing facility in Maharashtra, India.

The dashboard has been created as part of VVF's commitment towards environmental stewardship, biodiversity conservation, sustainability awareness, and employee engagement. It serves as a digital repository of documented flora and fauna species observed within the facility and aims to promote ecological awareness among employees, visitors, contractors, and stakeholders.

---

🌎 Project Objectives

* Document and showcase local biodiversity.
* Promote environmental awareness among employees.
* Support biodiversity conservation initiatives.
* Encourage citizen science and nature appreciation.
* Strengthen ESG and sustainability commitments.
* Provide a digital and QR-enabled biodiversity experience.

## 🌱 Biodiversity Highlights

The dashboard currently showcases:

* Birds
* Butterflies
* Reptiles
* Mammals
* Pollinators
* Native Trees
* Medicinal Plants
* Other Flora & Fauna

Each species profile includes:

* Common Name
* Local (Marathi) Name
* Scientific Name
* Ecological Significance
* Conservation Information
* Species Photographs

## 🌍 Why Biodiversity Matters

Biodiversity supports:

* Ecosystem stability
* Pollination
* Natural pest control
* Soil fertility
* Water conservation
* Climate resilience

Healthy biodiversity contributes significantly to sustainable industrial development and environmental protection.
